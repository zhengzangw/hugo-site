* Graph Isomorphism (GI)
  * Input: two undirected graphs $G$ and $H$
  * Output: $[G\cong H]$
* String Isomorphism (SI)
  * Input: two strings $x,y$: $[n]\rightarrow[m]$ and a permutation group $G\subseteq S_n$
  * Output: $[x\cong_G y]$
